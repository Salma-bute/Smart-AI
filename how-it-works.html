Technology Behind Smart Recycling

The AI system uses computer vision and deep learning neural networks trained on millions of waste images. Sensors detect material properties like density and composition. The system achieves 95% accuracy by analyzing visual features, weight, and material characteristics simultaneously.

Benefits for Schools

Smart recycling systems help schools reduce waste contamination by 30%, increase recycling rates by up to 50%, and educate students about sustainability. The technology provides real-time feedback, teaching proper recycling habits while making the process easy and accurate.
